# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 9, 2025**

## Overview

Doubling Season ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The App operates entirely on your device. All data created within the App (including tokens, decks, and settings) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information
- Usage data or analytics
- Location data
- Device information
- Crash reports or diagnostics
- Any other personal or non-personal information

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device using:
- Hive (local database)
- SharedPreferences (local settings storage)

This data remains on your device and is never synchronized to cloud services or external servers.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any data from any users, the App is safe for use by all ages.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
